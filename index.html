About Caldan Builders

Founded in 2008, Caldan Builders has grown to become one of Kenya's most trusted construction firms. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and timely delivery has earned us a reputation for excellence in both residential and commercial construction.

We specialize in sustainable building practices, utilizing the latest technologies and materials to create structures that are not only beautiful but also environmentally responsible and built to last.
